9 Days of Dusshera (Navratri)

  1. Day 1: Invocation – prayers and cleansing.
  2. Day 2: Strength – fasting and courage.
  3. Day 3: Wisdom – reflection and learning.
  4. Day 4: Harmony – cultural programs and balance.
  5. Day 5: Compassion – acts of giving.
  6. Day 6: Purity – self-improvement.
  7. Day 7: Devotion – bhajans and worship.
  8. Day 8: Victory – celebrating progress.
  9. Day 9: Celebration – Dussehra festival.
